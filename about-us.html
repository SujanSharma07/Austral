About Us

Welcome to Austral International Education Consultancy, your trusted partner in achieving your educational and migration goals. Located in Glenroy, Victoria, we specialize in providing comprehensive education and visa services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our experienced team is dedicated to helping students and professionals navigate the complexities of the Australian education and migration systems.

Our Mission

At Austral International Education Consultancy, we believe in the power of education and the opportunities it brings. We are committed to offering personalized guidance and support throughout your journey, from selecting the right course and institution to successfully obtaining your visa. Our goal is to make your transition to studying and living in Australia as smooth and rewarding as possible.

Whether you are seeking to advance your career, experience a new culture, or build a new life in Australia, we are here to assist you every step of the way.

Our Services

Education Services:

  • College and University Enrollment: We assist students in choosing the right educational institution and program that align with their career goals and academic interests. Our team provides end-to-end support, from application submission to securing admissions.
  • Course Selection Guidance: Expert advice on selecting courses that best fit your educational background and future aspirations.
  • Scholarship Assistance: Information and support to help you apply for scholarships and financial aid opportunities.

Visa Services:

  • Student Visas: Comprehensive guidance on the application process for student visas, ensuring you meet all requirements and deadlines.
  • Graduate Visas: Assistance for recent graduates seeking temporary or permanent residency in Australia.
  • Skilled Migration Visas: Support for skilled professionals looking to migrate to Australia through various visa pathways.
  • Family and Partner Visas: Help with family reunification and partner visa applications, ensuring a smooth and efficient process.

Migration Services:

  • Consultation and Assessment: Personalized consultations to assess your eligibility for different visa options and migration pathways.
  • Application Preparation and Lodgement: Expert assistance in preparing and lodging visa applications, ensuring all documentation is accurate and complete.
  • Post-Arrival Services: Support with settlement services, including finding accommodation, understanding Australian culture, and connecting with community resources.

Our Commitment

At Austral International Education Consultancy, we are dedicated to making your dreams of studying and living in Australia a reality. Trust us to provide reliable, professional, and friendly services tailored to your individual needs.
